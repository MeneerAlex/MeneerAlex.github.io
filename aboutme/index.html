Why do I like Miatas?

The Miatas popularity boils down to its fun driving experience, affordability, and reliability. Its lightweight design and rear-wheel-drive setup offer pure driving pleasure, while its price point makes it accessible to many. Even as prices rise, it remains one of the best value sports cars.
Beyond that, the Miatas customization options and active community are a big draw. With endless aftermarket support, owners can easily tweak performance or style. Its simple, timeless design also appeals to car enthusiasts who appreciate the look and feel of a classic roadster.
In short, the Miata is popular because it delivers everything a driver could want: fun, affordability, and endless potential.

Fun Facts about the Miata

  • Guinness World Record: The Mazda MX-5 Miata is the best-selling two-seater sports car of all time, with over one million units sold worldwide.
  • Weight and Design: The first-generation Miata (NA) weighs just over 2,100 pounds, which is part of the reason for its nimble handling and quick acceleration.
  • Inspired by British Roadsters: The Miata was inspired by classic British roadsters like the Lotus Elan and MGB, but with the added benefit of Japanese reliability.
  • Pop-Up Headlights Love: The first-generation Miata (NA) is famous for its iconic pop-up headlights, which have earned it a special place in car culture.
  • Affordable Performance: The Miata has long been known as the most affordable way to get behind the wheel of a true rear-wheel-drive sports car.
